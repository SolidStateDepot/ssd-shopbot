Shopbot tips

-World mode / joint
-Prefer MDI mode, and slow review of command input
-Be aware of G64 setting, tolerance that is too large will "cut-corners" and jeopardize fixutring, etc.
-Use a software stop when possible, E-stop when necessary
-The machine is not true
-Backlash is not well compensated
-Keep bystanders away from the machine
-Be aware of incremental (G91) vs absolute (G90) distance modes, misunderstanding can be catastrophic
-Be aware of your coordinate system, and the potential to accidentally command incorrect coordinate system motion
